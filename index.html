Our Vision and Mission

Vision: To deliver quality, cost effective and client-centred healthcare services in a secured and serene environment.

Mission: To promote quality and affordable healthcare through clinical practice and research by a highly skilled staff in a friendly environment. These would be done through: • Patient-centred care: Focus on providing excellent service and prioritizing patient’s needs.
• Quality care: Ensure access to high-quality healthcare services
• Innovation: Embrace innovative approaches to healthcare delivery.
• Collaboration: Foster partnerships with healthcare stakeholders





Our Core Values

General Practice Consultations

03

Professional Excellence

Our clinicians maintain the highest standards of skill and knowledge through regular training and continuing professional development.

We continuously seek better ways to care for our patients, embracing new medical advances, technology, and best practices to improve outcomes.

Our Facilities

Welcome to Sompa Medical Health Centre! From the moment you step through our doors, you’ll feel the warmth of a friendly, welcoming environment. We understand that visiting a clinic can be a little unsettling, so we’ve made it our mission to make you feel right at home. Our spacious reception area is furnished with comfortable, plush seating and tasteful decor in soothing colours. Here, you can relax as if you were in your own living room, yet know that you are in a top-quality medical facility.
